AUREON EXPLORER — what am I looking at?

This app makes hybrid search visible. Your documents become a 3D galaxy where position = meaning (nearby bubbles are about similar things). When you search, it shows not just what matched, but why — and how the engine decided.

What happens behind the scenes

01
Ingest
Files are split into paragraph-sized chunks — one bubble each.
02
Two searchers
BM25 matches exact words; LSA matches meaning.
03
Router
Reads your query and picks how much to trust each — the weight α.
04
Fuse & rank
14 fusion methods (score & rank based) combine the scores into the final ranking.

Want the numbers? ⚡ BENCHMARK ALL METHODS (left panel) scores every method on quality (nDCG, MRR, MAP, R-Prec, Recall, Precision) and efficiency (latency + QPS) on a labeled corpus.

Reading the scene

Blue bubble — matched on meaning (semantic / dense).
Orange bubble — matched on exact terms (keyword / sparse).
Bright star — your query, at the center of its top results.
Beams — links to the top hits; thicker & brighter = better score.
Dim bubble — a document that didn't score for this query.
Router gauge (bottom) — where α landed: SPARSE ↔ DENSE.
